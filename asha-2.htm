Ashalatha Kunnappilly

I am a PhD student at Mälardalen University and I am working in the Formal Modeling and Analysis of Embedded Systems group.

I have received a Master of Technology degree in Embedded Systems from Amrita University, India in June 2013 and a Bachelor of Technology degree in Electronics and Communication from Mahatma Gandhi University, India in June 2011. In December 2015, I have started my PhD studies under the supervision of Associate Professor Cristina Seceleanu, Postdoc Peter Backeman, and Professor Maria Lindén. My main research interest lies in the formal modeling and verification of cyber physical systems.
